The Virtual Reality

Virtual Reality or VR is a simulated 360-degree 9D environment that lets users explore and interact with scenes and objects that are designed to appear real. It’s an immersive experience that is rapidly capturing the households and businesses of today. VR technology is being used in medicine, entertainment, gaming, education, architecture, real estate and more. With its growing applications, VR development has become a sought-after skill that can take one’s career to the next dimension.
